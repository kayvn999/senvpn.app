3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Browsing history, URLs, or domain names accessed through VPN
  • Content of data transmitted through the VPN tunnel
  • Destination IP addresses of your connections
  • Precise geographic location (GPS)
  • Contacts, messages, photos, or files on your device
  • Credit card or payment information (handled entirely by Google Play)
VPN traffic is end-to-end encrypted — we cannot and do not read the content of your connections.

5. Advertising (AdMob)

Free accounts display ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may use Device ID and app usage data to serve personalized ads.

  • VIP accounts are completely ad-free
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in Android Settings → Google → Ads
Google Ads Policy: policies.google.com/technologies/ads
💎

6. VIP Subscription & Billing

  • Payments are processed entirely by Google Play Billing — we never store credit card information
  • Subscription status is managed by RevenueCat
  • Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the renewal date
  • Cancel anytime via Google Play → Subscriptions
🔒

7. Data Security

  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • VPN tunnel uses the OpenVPN protocol with AES-256 encryption
  • Account data is stored on Google Firebase with Google-standard security
  • We do not store passwords — authentication is handled via Google OAuth
🗓️

8. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you request account deletion
  • Connection history: Retained for up to 90 days
  • Crash logs: Retained for up to 90 days in Firebase Crashlytics
  • Analytics data: Retained per Google Analytics default settings (26 months)
